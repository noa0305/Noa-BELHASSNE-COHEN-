Circus in the city
Brussels
From an imposed site in Brussels, near the Gare du midi, design of a circus school. The project is based on the centralisation of the art of the circus, which makes it possible to work with an architecture similar to a hall.
Multi-family housing
Commune of Everre, Brussels
The CIEL (Conception Innovation Enseignement du Logement) workshop aims to to review the way of living. This project of 150 dwellings aimed to give value to collective spaces while working on innovative outdoor spaces that take into account a heterogeneous urban context mixing housing bars and garden cities.
